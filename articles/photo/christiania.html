A walk into mystery

Photo essay published in The University Times - 20th February 2017

Imagine a place, in the middle of Copenhagen, where the rules of the country are not applicable. Welcome to Freetown Christiania, where a policeman will not enter the place, in accordance with an unspoken agreement.

“Freedom” is a word that covers really well the spirit of the independent city. One particularity is that drugs are sold legally. You will meet dealers, without hoodies or black sunglasses, selling their products in the street or on a bench.

“Christiania is a dwelling for people who wish to live in a different manner” - Christian Wedell-Neergaard
